Meet the Doctor

Combining a gentle approach with surgical precision, Dr. Pragya Singh restores healthy gums and confident smiles. Her passion for patient education ensures you understand every step of your periodontal journey.

A proud MDS Gold Medalist, Dr. Singh stays at the forefront of periodontology through continuous learning and global conferences.

Outside the clinic, she mentors budding dentists and volunteers in community oral‑health programs across NCR.
